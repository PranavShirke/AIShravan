Sign Language Gestures

Hello
How to Sign:
  • Start with your hand near your forehead
  • Extend your fingers and thumb
  • Move your hand outward in a small wave
  • Keep your palm facing forward
Usage: Greeting someone
Goodbye
How to Sign:
  • Start with your hand near your face
  • Extend your fingers and thumb
  • Move your hand outward in a waving motion
  • Keep your palm facing the person
Usage: Saying farewell
Yes
How to Sign:
  • Make a fist with your hand
  • Move your hand up and down
  • Like nodding your head
  • Keep your palm facing inward
Usage: Affirming something
No
How to Sign:
  • Start with your fingers together
  • Quickly snap them apart
  • Like shaking your head
  • Keep your palm facing forward
Usage: Denying something
I Love You
How to Sign:
  • Raise your hand with palm facing outward
  • Extend your thumb, index, and pinky fingers
  • Keep your middle and ring fingers down
  • Hold the position steady
Usage: Expressing love
Help
How to Sign:
  • Make a fist with one hand
  • Place it on your open palm
  • Move both hands upward together
  • Keep your palms facing up
Usage: Requesting assistance
Sorry
How to Sign:
  • Make a fist with your right hand
  • Place it on your chest
  • Move it in a circular motion
  • Keep your palm facing inward
Usage: Apologizing
Fear
How to Sign:
  • Open both hands with fingers spread
  • Place them near your chest
  • Move them outward quickly
  • Keep your palms facing inward
Usage: Expressing fear
